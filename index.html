Who is my favorite artist

This week we're tasked with looking at who is our favorite artist. For me it was a question where the answer was simple, it was explaining it that was difficult. My favorite living artist is Kanye West.

Why Kanye West

Kanye West emerged at a time when musicions were musicians, and rarely did they merge into the lane of "Artist" This was especially true for hip hop artist of the time. Hip hop was a relatively new art form (and at the time was fighting for legitimacy as an art form) that was dominated by overthe top but one dimensional rappers. Very rarely were these rappers taken seroiusly as "artist" Kanye West emerged from Chicago as a producer turned rapper who was confident and unwavvering in his ability and desire to be taken seriously as an artist in all facets.

About Kanye West

Kanye West, rapper, singer, producer, and entrepreneur was born in Atlanta, Georgia on June 8, 1977 to Donda and Ray West. Donda was an English professor, and Ray, a former Black Panther, was an award winning photojournalist. West married Kim Kardashian in 2014, and the now divorced couple have multiple children together. West has had more than 50 Grammy nominations, and has taken home over 20 trophies.

Discography

When it comes to music Kanye West has released 10 offiaial solo studio albums in his career. West didn't however get his start rapping. His initial forray into music was producing beats for other rappers. His discography is as followed

  1. Colleg Dropout
  2. Late Registration
  3. Graduation
  4. 808sandheartbreaks
  5. My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy
  6. Yeezus
  7. The life of Pablo
  8. YE
  9. Jesus is King
  10. Donda
For West his albums all touch on different themes both musically and personally. His first album was lauded as a refreshing hip hop classic. It infused his trademark production style (sped up soul samples) over bragadocious but concious and self aware raps. From there he pushed into the follow up Late Registration. West was so serious about his work he used 2 million dollars of his own money to fund a live Orchestra to preform. Graduation was a triumphant album West intended to be played in Stadiums. He learned alot from traveling in Europe, but this album also marked a changing of the guard. West challanged and beat rap juggernaut 50 Cent in a battle for first week sales. 808s was an album West recorded in Hawaii (hence the 808s, but also the drum machine) after the death of his mother, and his engagement was called off. My beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy was an album Kanye also made in Hawaii but among differnt circumstances. West was basically exiled from music after an incident with Taylor Swift at the VMAs. He went to Hawaii and assembled a dream team of frequent collaborators and set out to make the perfect album. West told the New York Times, " “Let me show you guys what I can do, and please accept me back. You want to have me on your shelves” This album is widely regarded as his magnum opus. Next for West was YEEZUS, his most divisive albumm. If MBDTF was a love letter to hip hop and him asking for acceptence, YEEZUS was a messy chaotic breakup and rebelious streak saying "i don't need tou you need me" Yeezus wa an industrial minimalist electronic sounding album. The verses were shorter and edgier, the sound shocking. Kanye began to dip into fashion even ranting about then YSL creative director Heidi Slimaine. West released The Life of Pablo next and intertwined it with his growing fashion line. His next album YE was apart of a summer flurry of albums he produced, and was also accomapnied by an album with long time collaborator kid cudi entitled "Kids see Ghost" Kanye overwent a Christian transition, and his music reflected it. He began doing "sunday service" performances with remakes of pop songs with Christian Themes. His next album YE was a kanye album, but with full on Christian themes. The album went on to won the Grammy for Best contemporary Christian Album. Kanye's most recent work is DONDA. A sprawling over one hour long album with Christian Themes that also loosely serves a tribute to his late mother.

Work outside of music

Outside of music Kanye has branched into a few different areas of Art that make him an exceptional talent. Before becoming a musicion, West was making a name for himself in the Art World. In high school West won a few art contest, and earned a scholarship to Chicago’s American Academy of Art. West also has made a name for himself in Fashion. In the late 2000s West flirted with launching a few now defunct fashion brands and also interened at Fendi. In 2009 Kanye did see the release of the Nike Air Yeezy. The next decade would see Kanye release the air Yeezy 2 and leave nike. When West Left Nike he took his talents to Adidas and also took his newfound Fashion Brand YEEZY. YEEZY is now valeud at over a Billion dollars and has had shows in New York Fashion Week. He's also worked with GAP and A.P.C. and Louis Vuitton amongh other companies.

Influence

To look at Kanye West Influnece as an ARTIST is asking two different questions, his direct influence and his indirect influence. His direct influnece would be the moments he directly helped create, this could be something like creating "takeover" for Jay-z. This is readily apparent and easy to notice. What's not as aparent is his indirect influence. This could be attributed to things like how West dressed when he first emerged. It could be extended to the fact that his emotional and personal vulnerability in music spilled over into other musicians at the time. Artist from The Weeknd, to Drake, to Travis Scott, to Bon Iver and the Chain Smokers site him as an influence. His use of fahion and things like Shutter Shades were ubiquitous. West fingerprints have been on Culture for two decades now.
